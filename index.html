Mission

AquaAI's purpose.

  • Save more than 1,600,000 lives of endangered aquatic species every year.
  • Rescue critically endangered species from the brink of extinction & preserve biodiversity.
  • Eradicate unsustainable commercial fishing practices with strict enforcement.
  • Raise awareness about the preservation efforts of endangered aquatic species.
Product

AquaAI — the intelligent way to save our oceans.

  • Mimics animals' sounds, releases pheromones, and visually deters endangered aquatic species away from fishing nets.
  • Range of 100km+, speeds exceeding 200km/h.
  • Communicates with solar-powered access points using sonar.
  • 1500 pods covering an area of 47 million square kilometres.
  • Users can interact with AquaAI in real-time via an app.
